Our Story

We are two students living in Tallahassee, Florida who absolutely love the outdoors. Growing up in the flat, tropical region of South Florida, we were enthralled when we moved to Tallahassee and saw the hills and forests that surrounded us. After three years learning more each time we traveled around our surrounding region, we realized that there needed to be a resource for Tallahassee residents, college students, and visitors alike to easily find what outdoor activities were available to them. Fast forward a few months later, and here we are!

Happy Trailblazing!
