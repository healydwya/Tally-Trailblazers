Indian Mounds State Park

Indian Mounds State Park is a historic site in Northeast Tallahassee where ancient mounds built by Native Tallahassee men and women can be found and viewed. There are clearly marked hiking trails surrounding the park, and lots of fun places to climb up and view the sights.
