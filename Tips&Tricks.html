Tips & Tricks

  1. Don't forget sunscreen
  2. Wear long pants and boots if you are hiking somewhere there may be ticks or poison ivy
  3. Make sure you bring enough water and food if you will be out for a long time
  4. Bring a buddy or friend so that you'll be safe and alert out on the trails
  5.  

& Most importantly, have fun!
